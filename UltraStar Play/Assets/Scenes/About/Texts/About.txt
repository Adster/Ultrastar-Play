UltraStar Play is a free and open source sing-along karaoke game.
It enables you to sing along to your favorite music in order to score points, depending on the pitch of the voice and the rhythm of singing.

This project is developed as community effort on GitHub.
Please consider contributing to this project, for example by submitting bug reports, art assets, sounds, art assets or source code.
